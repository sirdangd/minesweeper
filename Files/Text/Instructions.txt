The instructions are:

1. click on any square to start game, doesn't matter which one to start
2. a bunch of numbers and empty spaces, or just one number will appear where you click. Those numbers are called neighbors
3. the neighbors depict how many mines are touching that square (up, down, left, right, and diagonals)
4. if you are certain that there is a mine under a certain square, right click on it to flag it, once flagged, square can not be clicked, to get rid of flag, click the middle mouse button
5. to win, all squares must be clicked revealed, and mines must be flagged

HAVE FUN!
Remember, this is a game of chance, or strategy, your choice ;)
